Devils Food Cake!

This is a quick example HTML project rebuilding the recipe collaboration of Cakes by Shivesh.This Devils Food Cake Recipe vedio was created by shivesh Bhatiya and presented on his Youtube channel

Ingredients

For the cake
  • 1 ½ cup(180gms) maida

  • ¾ cup(90gms) cocoa powder

  • ½ tsp(3gms) baking soda

  • 1 tsp (4gms) baking powder

  • ¾ cup(180ml) vegetable oil

  • 1 ½ cup(300gms) caster sugar

  • 1 cup(285gms) curd

  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

  • ½ cup(120ml) milk + 1tsp(5ml) vinegar
For chocolate fudge frosting
  • 1 cup(200gms) butter

  • 2 cups(260gms) icing sugar

  • ½ to ¾ cup cocoa powder

  • 1 cup(175gms) melted chocolate

  • 1 cup(230ml) warm cream

Preparations

  1. First combine some milk and vinegar,mix it and set aside for few minutes.
  2. Sift the dry ingrdients including maida,baking soda and cocoa powder.
  3. Now start working on your wet ingredients,whisk sugar and vegetable oil together very well,add curd and splash of vanilla extract.whisk everything together.
  4. Start adding the dry mix into the wet ingredients in three batches with buttermilk.Putting it together in three batches.
  5. pour it into the pan with parchment paper.
  6. Bake this in preheated oven at 180° C for about 30-35 mins.
  7. For the frosting,take 1 cup of butter and beat this at high speed.Now sift in icing sugar and cocoa powder.
  8. Beat the frosting at high speed using electric mixer until its light and fluffy.
  9. add 1 cup melted dark chocolate and fresh cream and mix it.
  10. Keep the mixture into the fridge for about 10 mins.
  11. At th end, spread generous amount of the chocolate fudge frosting.
